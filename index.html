What is Dungeons & Dragons?

Dungeons & Dragons is a tabletop role-playing game. Players take on a fantasy persona and act as them in a world conceived and controlled by a Dungeon Master. The Dungeon Master (DM) is a role taken on by one player and controls the game for everyone else. The DM creates the world, the non-player characters (NPCs), the monsters, and the narrative that the player characters (PCs) will be playing through.
