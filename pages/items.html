Items and Encounters

Items and Encounters are the core of the game play. The game is set up as a choose-your-own-adventure narrative so as the Dungeon Master (DM) you'llbe in charge of filling the world so it feels realistic. Everyone who is not a player character (PC) is a DM controlled non-player character (NPC). This includes denizens, villains, and most importantly: MONSTERS.
