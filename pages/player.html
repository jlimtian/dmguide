Player Characters

As important as the Dungeon Master (DM) is, the real stars of the show are the player characters (PC). The ultimate goal in the game is to make sure that everyone is having fun. There are many things a DM can do to make the player experience as easy and efficient as possible to make sure there is plenty of room for fun. This process starts before even the game. I find it helpful to make and distribute a chart that players can fill in with information about the character they're making for the campaign. A good DM will make sure they collect as much detail as possible about the player and their CHARACTER.
