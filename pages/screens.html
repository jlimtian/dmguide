Behind the Screens

Now that all that prep is done, it's time for the hard part! Research and gathering information is easy, now it's time to put it to practice. Even though this is the hardest part of the game it's arguably the most fun. Now that the game is in session and is live there are things you, as the Dungeon Master, absolutely need to keep track of in real time. Here are only some of the most important things a DM must keep track of behind the SCREEN:

  • Antagonist and their interactions with players
  • Player characters derailing the campaign
  • Combat and non-combat encounters
  • Following and making up rules, taking notes, music, figurines, etc
  • Random and important NPCs
  • Travel time, currency, location economy and culture
  • Voices, mannerisms, value, etc. of all NPCs that fill the world
