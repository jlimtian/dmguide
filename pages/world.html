World Building

Developing your world is one of the most important parts of immersion. If your players don't feel that the world they're traversing is is real there won't feel like there are stakes nor will they feel the weight of consequences. That will result in a stale player experience and can cause the game to get boring and stagnant. There are ways to prevent this. Fleshing out general locations like kingdoms, regions, and cities go a long way. If not in intricate detail, at least have some defining characteristic unique to the locations, for example: rude people, fishing town, plague ridden, etc. Another way to add immersion is to provide your players with a MAP.
